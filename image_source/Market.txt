Market:

The Market is the place to go for new units and buildings. You can buy them by speding your experience points. Once you buyed an new item it will appear in the user interface in the game. 

Please not that it can be possible that you need different buildings and/or  construction vehicle to be able to build some of the units you can buy in the market.

Experience Points:

There are two ways to get experience points:

1:	You will get experience points for every unit and building every 10 minutes.As long as your 	units and buildings are not destroyed, you get points for them.

2.	Destroy units and buildings of other players. 

Please note that you continue to get points while your not logged in to the game. You can come back to the game if you have a registred user. Please register here if you don't have a user yet.
